How Mail My CV works?

We looks for job openings from various companies and short these openings in categories.

Step 1


Choose a package of your choice and make the payment online using your credit or debit card. You are all done!

Step 2

Receive candidate login credential, after logged-in attach your cv, select lists employer,Add subject of email (for example: Accountant (3 Years UAE experienced) with Driving License and NOC,Add cover letter in email content (ask if you need sample)

Step 3

Send to employer, you can watch your sending report including open mail, bounce mail etc...
